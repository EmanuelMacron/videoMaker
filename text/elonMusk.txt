Did you know that Elon Musk the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, is a college dropout!
He left the University of Pretoria in South Africa after just two days and later dropped out of the University of Pennsylvania after two years.
He then moved to Silicon Valley and co-founded web software company Zip2, which was eventually sold for almost $300 million.
He also founded X.com, an online payment company, which later became PayPal and was sold to eBay for $1.5 billion.
He's also a visionary entrepreneur and inventor, who has founded companies like SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company.
