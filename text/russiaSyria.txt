Have you heard about this Russian Federation's war crime? In september 2016, According to reports from human rights organizations, Russian forces targeted a residential neighborhood in Aleppo, bombing several buildings and causing the death of at least 50 civilians, including women and children. The attack also resulted in the destruction of a nearby school, putting the education of countless children at risk.

Eyewitnesses have described the horror of the scene, with rubble and debris strewn across the streets and families torn apart by the indiscriminate bombing. Rescuers struggled to reach those trapped under the rubble, with the lack of access to medical care leaving many with serious injuries.

In response to these allegations, the Russian government issued a statement denying any responsibility for the attack and claiming that their forces only targeted military targets in the area.
