Did you know that Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson made his first million as a professional wrestler? He signed with the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) in 1996 and quickly became one of the biggest stars in the company. He was known for his charismatic personality and catchphrases, as well as his impressive in-ring skills. He was also one of the most marketable wrestlers, with merc and sponsorship deals that helped him earn a lot of money.
Eventually, he transitioned to acting and become one of the highest-paid actors in Hollywood.
